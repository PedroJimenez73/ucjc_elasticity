The increase in the price of a product affects in different ways its decrease in sales depending on how necessary the product is.

For example, if the price of a car doubles without providing new features, its sales will drop drastically, while if the good is a necessity, even if its price rises, sales will not be affected.

Hence we speak of elastic and inelastic goods, but, how does the market react to a good being elastic or inelastic?
