1 Price elasticity on demand

1.1 How shapes of supply and demand curves matter

The shapes of the supply and demand curves determine by how much a shock affects the equilibrium price and quantity.

You can see an example of the importance of this curve in avocado production. The supply of avocados depends on the price of avocados and the price of fertilizer, a major input in producing them.
