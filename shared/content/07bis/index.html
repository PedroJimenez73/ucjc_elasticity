1.2 Sensitivity of quantity demanded to price

Elasticity

The percentage change in a variable in response to a given percentage change in another variable.

Price elasticity of demand (Îµ)

The percentage change in the quantity demanded in response to a given percentage change in the price, at a particular point on the demand curve.

Mathematical representation

Elasticity is always negative, so sometimes a negative sign is put in front to work with positive numbers.
