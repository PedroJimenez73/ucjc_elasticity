1.3 Price elasticity of demand

Elasticity is sometimes thought to be constant throughout a demand function. Is not always that way. For example, in a linear demand function, the elasticity varies between 0 and infinity.

Let's see why...

Elasticity has two elements: the inverse of the slope of the demand function, which is a constant (ΔQ / Δp) and the element p / Q (which varies throughout the function).
